About Intellect.ScholarHub

We built Intellect.ScholarHub because quality academic and legal research tools should be free, accurate, and accessible to every researcher — regardless of institution, location, or language.

🎯 Our Mission

Intellect.ScholarHub exists to democratize academic and legal research tools. In 2025, the best citation generators are locked behind paywalls. The best legal research tools cost hundreds of dollars per month. This is unacceptable for students in developing countries, independent researchers, and solo practitioners who deserve the same quality tools as researchers at Harvard or attorneys at elite law firms.

We built a platform with 22 AI-powered tools — completely free — that handles everything from APA 7th edition citations to IRAC legal briefs to PhD dissertation planning. Powered by Claude AI, the most accurate language model available for academic and legal contexts.

🌍 Who We Serve

  • PhD & Doctoral Researchers: Literature review generation, dissertation chapter planning, research question development
  • Law Students & Attorneys: IRAC/FIRAC legal brief generation, legal argument building, contract clause drafting
  • Undergraduate Students: APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE, Harvard citation generators for any source type
  • Academic Researchers: Literature reviews, systematic review frameworks, peer review assistance
  • Arabic-Speaking Researchers: Full bilingual platform with RTL support — the only comprehensive tool of its kind

🤖 Our Technology

All 22 tools are powered by Claude AI (claude-sonnet-4-20250514) by Anthropic — widely recognized as the most accurate AI model for nuanced, technical content including academic citations and legal analysis.

Unlike rule-based citation generators that apply rigid templates, our AI understands context. It correctly handles unusual source types, catches common formatting errors, and explains its output so you learn while you cite.

Privacy commitment: Your API key is stored locally in your browser only. We never see it, store it, or transmit it. No account required. No data collection.
